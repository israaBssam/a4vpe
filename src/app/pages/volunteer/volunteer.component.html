​At the core of our mission is our ability to bring together American and Palestinian businesspeople by providing the information and context they each need to team up successfully. The key to this strategy is AVPE’s network in the U.S. and Palestine.

We call on our robust and engaged network to assist us in connecting Palestinian and American companies – with sector information or contacts, expertise, or other types of detailed data. By joining our network you are joining a global community of people who support the growth and development of an independent a nd free Palestinian econom

​How can you join this network?

​Sign up for our email list. We then send you regular updates on our work (our newsletter) and calls to action that detail specific requests we have from Palestinian companies. We ask you share your relevant expertise with us or, alternatively, link us to people in your network who may be able to offer assistance.

At AVPE we are a strong as the network we can build and each and every one of you is a part of that. Encourage people you know to join our email list and help us expand our reach and therefore our ability to fulfill requests from Palestinian companies. You can also support us by following us on our social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and YouTube).

​Join our network and help us fight for economic freedom for Palestine!
