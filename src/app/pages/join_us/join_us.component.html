Shifting Perspectives: Resetting U.S.-Palestine Business Opportunities

​AVPE looks to the future with our eyes wide open, our hearts engaged, and a continued commitment to work toward a vibrant Palestinian economy. 2020 brought uncertainty and ‘new normals’ for the whole world. Today, we look to the future with hope for improved U.S.-Palestine relations and a new level of economic engagement between Palestinian and American businesspeople. AVPE is here to support Palestinians companies turn a difficult year into opportunities for the future

Your gift supports the work of AVPE to shift perspectives and reset American-Palestinian business opportunities.

Give generously and support Palestinian businesspeople to better face the challenges of both COVID-19 and Israeli occupation. Your dollars help to connect Palestinian businesses with resources and know-how from the U.S. while simultaneously providing Americans a more informed image that appreciates the patience, resilience, and business savvy of the Palestinian people.

Americans for a Vibrant Palestinian Economy (AVPE) is registered in the USA as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Contributions to AVPE are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. AVPE's tax identification number is 47-1500245.
